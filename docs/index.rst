Take Home Pay Calculator: Calculate Your Net Salary After Taxes (2026)
=====================================================================

A **Take Home Pay Calculator** helps you calculate your estimated net salary after taxes and deductions. Enter your gross income, pay frequency, tax details, and deductions to find out how much money you will receive in your paycheck.

What Is a **Take Home Pay Calculator**?
---------------------------------------

A **Take Home Pay Calculator** estimates your net pay by subtracting taxes and payroll deductions from your gross salary. It helps you understand your actual income after deductions.

How to Use the **Take Home Pay Calculator**
-------------------------------------------

Follow these steps:

#. Enter your gross salary or hourly wage.
#. Select your pay schedule.
#. Choose your filing status.
#. Add state and tax details.
#. Enter deductions if applicable.
#. Click the **Calculate** button to view your estimated take-home pay.

What Does the Calculator Include?

The calculation may include:

* Gross income.

* State taxes.
* Social Security tax.
* Medicare tax.
* Retirement contributions.
* Health insurance deductions.
* Other payroll deductions.
